  1. Fix survey completion rate — target 25%+ response rate. Current 11.5% makes CSAT data unreliable for decisions. Enable automated follow-up emails and consider in-portal rating prompts. Owner: CS Ops
  2. Investigate and resolve the PSA Known Issues 0% solve rate. 20 tickets routed here with no closures. Either tickets are misrouted, the group is a staging queue, or there's a workflow blocker. Audit immediately. Owner: CS Director
  3. Build account-level Zendesk + Salesforce bridge. 88% of tickets have org_id but no account name. Join to Salesforce to enable churn correlation — identify accounts with high ticket volume + long resolution times as early churn signals. Owner: RevOps / Revay
  4. Set FRT SLAs by tier and enforce in Zendesk triggers. Average FRT of 28.4h is high. High-value accounts should have <4h FRT SLA with escalation automation. Use Zendesk triggers to auto-escalate breaches. Owner: CS Ops
  5. Audit the top 5 high-risk accounts (20+ tickets/mo). Cross-reference with renewal dates in Salesforce. Assign dedicated CSMs to accounts with 15+ open tickets or resolution times >48h. Owner: CSM Team
  6. Standardize tagging taxonomy. Current tags are inconsistent and overlap (e.g. multiple billing-related tags). A clean taxonomy enables better routing, forecasting, and product feedback loops to Engineering/PM. Owner: CS Ops / Product
