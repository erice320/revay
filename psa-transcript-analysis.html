Onboarding Methodology prescriptive vs. open-ended — three-stage signal analysis
Three-stage methodology framework: Ideal onboarding is expert-led, not blank-slate. We score SA behavior at three distinct stages because the same phrase signals differently depending on project position. A discovery question in call 1 signals the sales handoff failed. That same question in call 6 of 8 signals project drift. Scores are derived from real language patterns across 530 transcripts and 255 accounts — not editorial judgment.
EARLY  Calls 1–2 · Did the SA arrive with context, or start from zero?
MID  Middle calls · Is the SA recommending, or asking the client to decide?
LATE  Final 2 calls · Is there a graduation date, or is the project drifting?
