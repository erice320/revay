🚀 Key Findings & Recommendations

1. 🚨 Onboarding capacity crisis — act now
2,368 open tickets, 56-day average resolution, and no sign of capacity relief. Options: (a) add SA headcount, (b) segment tickets into tier-1 self-serve vs. hands-on SA, (c) build "known issues" content to deflect repeat submissions. Inaction here will convert to churn in Q2–Q3 as onboarded clients hit steady-state frustration.
2. 🚨 Investigate Rev.io Payments (0% solve rate)
356 tickets with zero resolutions in 5 months is not a performance issue — it's a process or routing failure. This needs a 15-minute conversation with whoever owns Payments CS. Either close the queue properly, add the resolution step to the workflow, or redirect tickets to an active queue.
3. ⚠️ Fix PSA AM quality gap before it becomes churn
67% CSAT and 31% unsolved rate in PSA AM with 459 accounts and 3 AMs (one not even in Zendesk). Document exactly which ticket types are going unresolved. Consider whether AM capacity or tooling is the bottleneck. This is the highest-risk gap for PSA expansion.
4. ⚠️ Activate the Client Churn queue
The group exists but has 0 tickets. Define routing rules: any cancellation request, any AM-escalated at-risk account, any account in the 90d+ ticket age bucket routes here. This creates a trackable, measurable early-warning system instead of relying on gut feel.
5. ⚠️ CLOUDLI account review before March QBR season
133 tickets at 20-day average resolution = frustrated client. Pair this with their MRR (pending NetSuite) to assess risk. If MRR is significant, assign a dedicated escalation path. If MRR is low and cost is high, a different conversation may be warranted.
6. ✅ Codify the Cameron Resuta / JB Hendrix standard
100% CSAT, 90–96h resolution, 500+ ticket volume. This is the benchmark. Document their triage approach, response templates, and escalation patterns. Use it for coaching Sean Kuta (396h avg) and any incoming agents. This single action could move team-wide resolution time by 20–30%.
7. 📊 Fix data infrastructure for next cycle
Three gaps blocking better analysis: (a) Get agent/admin Zendesk credentials for fresh pulls + FRT data, (b) Connect NetSuite MRR for revenue-weighted account risk, (c) Make CSM Sentiment field required on AM tickets. These three changes unlock a significantly more powerful CS health dashboard in 30 days.
