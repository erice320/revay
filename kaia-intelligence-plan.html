Step 1

Outreach → S3

Outreach Kaia's Daily Export feature writes all transcripts and call metadata as structured files to an AWS S3 bucket every 24 hours. Covers every call across all reps automatically.

Step 2

Process + Enrich

A nightly job reads new S3 files, parses transcripts, links Outreach opportunity IDs to Salesforce records, and stores structured data in a local database.

Step 3

Analyze + Alert

Query transcripts by keyword, topic, rep, or deal. Run pattern analysis. Generate reports. Trigger alerts when specific language appears — competitor mentions, pricing objections, missing features.

Data

What We Capture Per Call

From Kaia / Outreach

Call Intelligence

  • Full transcript (speaker-labeled, timestamped)
  • Meeting date, duration, format (call/meeting)
  • Participants: reps + prospects (names, roles)
  • Outreach Opportunity ID (native link)
  • Outreach Account ID
  • Kaia AI topics (aggregated topics discussed)
  • Call state (completed, dropped, etc.)
  • Provider (Zoom, Teams, Twilio, etc.)
From Salesforce (via API)

Deal Context

  • Opportunity stage & stage at time of call
  • Loss reason + detail notes
  • Product type (PSA 2.0, Billing, etc.)
  • Total MRR / ARR value
  • Close date (actual or projected)
  • Account name, industry, size
  • Assigned rep + manager
  • Competitor (if "Lost to Competitor")

Key linkage: Outreach natively stores the opportunity_id on every Kaia recording. This is the bridge — no fuzzy name-matching needed. Every transcript links cleanly to a Salesforce opportunity and inherits all its deal metadata.

Use Cases

What Becomes Possible

🔍 Feature Gap Intelligence

Scan all lost-deal transcripts for what prospects actually said about missing features. Surface patterns product can act on — ranked by MRR impact.

"Show me every call in the last 90 days where a prospect mentioned QuickBooks, ranked by deal size"

🔁 Re-engagement Triggers

When a feature ships in a new release, automatically identify all past closed-lost deals where that feature was mentioned in a transcript. Alert the rep to re-engage.

"Prepaid Support Hour Billing just shipped → flag all deals where block hours or prepaid contracts came up"

📊 Rep Coaching & QA

Analyze talk ratios, topic coverage, objection handling patterns by rep. Identify which reps consistently surface capabilities early vs. letting blockers surface late.

"Which reps ask about accounting software in the first call vs. discovery call 3+"

⚠️ Competitive Intelligence

Flag every call where ConnectWise, Autotask, Simpro, or other competitors are mentioned. Track which competitors are showing up most in active pipeline vs. lost deals.

"Show me all open PSA deals where ConnectWise was mentioned in the last 30 days"

📈 Deal Health Signals

Detect language patterns that correlate with deals going dark — "we need to check internally," "let me bring this back to leadership," long gaps with no follow-up calls.

"Show open opps where the last call included stall language and no call has been scheduled"
