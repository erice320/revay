🤖 AI & Agentic Efficiency Opportunities

This analysis examines which ticket categories represent genuine opportunities for AI and agentic automation versus work that requires human judgment. Using tag, resolution time, and category data from 7,218 SLA tickets, we've quantified the deflection opportunity and tiered it by what's technically achievable today.

Automation Tiers

🟢 Tier 1 — Fully Autonomous
AI handles end-to-end • No human in the loop

Password resets, account lookups, profile changes, credential resets, standard report delivery. Fast, repeatable, zero judgment required.

🔵 Tier 2 — AI-Drafted, Human-Approved
AI drafts • Human reviews & sends

Config changes, billing questions, custom report requests. Cuts resolution time from hours to minutes while keeping a human sign-off.

🟡 Tier 3 — AI-Assisted
AI surfaces context • Human does the work

Complex troubleshooting, PSA database issues, invoicing questions. AI surfaces similar past tickets and suggested next steps. Makes CSMs faster, not redundant.

🔴 Tier 4 — Human Only
No AI substitution

Relationship work, churn risk conversations, contract negotiations, emotionally escalated clients. Human judgment and empathy are non-negotiable here.
