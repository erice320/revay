The 5× Thesis PSA Web launched into a "bridge market" — security integrators and AV companies who would buy an early-stage product. That got us to 115 wins and $75K MRR. The next 5× requires three things: fix the execution machine (show rates, rep coaching, SDR quality, paid media ROI), monetize near-term pipeline (billing cross-sell, Apollo 2 re-engagements), and build the IT MSP competitive displacement motion that is the long-term growth engine. The product is ready. It requires changing how — and to whom — we sell it.

  • Nearly all paid spend (Meta) was focused on commercial security/AV — a small, blue-collar, field-based buyer who is chronically unavailable and the first meeting to get bumped
  • IT MSPs running ConnectWise, Kaseya, Halo, or NinjaOne are the true ICP — higher ACV, better retention, product-market fit exists

✅ Decision: Media Shift — In Progress

Shift 80%+ of paid media from Meta → LinkedIn (majority), X, and Reddit — IT MSP focused. Already started as of March 2. Team is rebuilding the account and campaigns now; first ad goes live within the week. Will take time to ramp and optimize.

Note: Existing site content naturally indexing for Integrators stays — no cost to maintain. No new work or ad spend will be funded to that audience going forward.

⚠️ Expect a short-term pipeline dip as transition resets top-of-funnel. This is expected and acceptable.
